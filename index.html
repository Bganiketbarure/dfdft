ABOUT COMPANY

BVNAB Intelligence is a technology-driven company specializing in industrial automation, robotics, AI vision, and Industry 4.0 solutions to improve efficiency and productivity. We deliver end-to-end services including automation systems, PLC/SCADA, software, and IIoT across industries like Manufacturing, Automobile, Pharma, and Packaging. Along with industrial solutions, we support education through STEM services, prototyping, and research initiatives. We also conduct hands-on training in robotics, automation, AI/ML, and advanced technologies. Our goal is to bridge the gap between industry and education through practical innovation and smart solutions.
